In my current role as an Operational Officer at Princess Alexandra Hospital- Group Linen Services since May 2022, I excel in delivering comprehensive linen services.
My expertise includes machine-assisted folding and ironing, meticulous hand-folding of linen, and expert packaging of cleaned linen.
Prior to this, from January 2015 to May 2022, I served as a Chiller Assessor and MSA Beef Grader at Australian Country Choice.
In this role, I conducted detailed evaluations of beef carcasses following MSA guidelines. This involved assessing various attributes such as marbling, meat color, fat cover, and ossification.
I actively collaborated with a dedicated team to ensure precise and consistent grading in line with MSA standards. My contributions extended beyond evaluation; I provided valuable feedback to producers and suppliers, enhancing beef quality and achieving superior grading results.
Managing precise grading records and collaborating with different departments for data analysis were integral aspects of my responsibilities.
Through my efforts, I significantly contributed to maintaining product excellence and ensuring customer satisfaction by implementing effective grading protocols.
I am passionate about delivering exceptional quality in every task I undertake, and I am committed to continuous improvement and excellence in all aspects of my work.
